Hi, I'm Jennifer! I'm a student at the University of Michigan studying Data Science and Computer Science.

I spend most of my time leading and volunteering for the Society of Women Engineers, where I plan social events, maintain our online presence, and organize a conference for women in leadership.

I'm also interested in research involving education, and the potential for technology to advance education. In high school, I researched executive functions and their ability to predict learning outcomes.

In my free time, I also love to read, write, and practice taekwondo.
